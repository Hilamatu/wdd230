Visual Hierarchy

DC Shoes

dcshoes.com

When you open the webpage, the biggest thing and the first thing that catches your attention is the picture of the guy snowboarding. Since the winter is coming and the snowboarding season will start soon, they are leading the visitors to their snowboarding products so I believe that the principle of Visual Hierarchy is well exemplified on this webpage.

Hick's Law

Plan B

planbskateboards

The plan B brand is a brand that is focused on skateboard decks but they also sell skateboard wheels and accessories and they have decks of different types and sizes. But on their webpage, you can apply the filter search by product, by size, by series, and by riders since they have pro model decks. So, the Hick's law is well applied here.

Fitt's Law

Street League Skateboarding

streetleague.com

The Street League Skateboarding is a website with a lot of information. you have videos, resutls from past competition, when and where will be the next tour, and you can buy your tickets. the main focus of them is to sell the tickets and you can see the Fitt's Law well applied with the buttons "Coming soon" and "Buy now" because the button's color is different and the letters are capitalized, making it easier to find and to hit.
