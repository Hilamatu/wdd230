The Kickflip

There are many tricks that can be performed on a skateboard divided by category. Today we are going to takl about flip tricks. A flip trick is a type of skateboarding trick in which the skateboard rotates around its vertical axis, or its vertical axis and its horizontal axis simultaneously. The first flip trick, called a kickflip but originally known as a "magic flip", was invented by professional skateboarder Rodney Mullen. A kickflip is when a skateboarder flips the board 360 degrees on its horizontal axis by flicking the corner of the board towards the backside of the skater. Instances of multiple spins are named according to how many spins are completed (e.g. double kickflip, triple kickflip, etc). From kickflip a huge list of flip tricks were created. the most common are Heelflip (Similar to a kickflip, the heelflip is instead executed with the heel section flipping away from the skater this time.), Varial kickflip ( kickflip combined with a backside-pop shuvit) and, my favorite trick, 360 flip/tre flip (A combination of a 360-degree backside-pop shuvit and a kickflip.) and it was also invented by the king Rodney Mullen. There are more flip tricks but this is it for today.
